I was recently interviewed for a facilitator role for the 1st Student Leaders' Summit, my school's new flagship event, and I was 
quite taken aback by their interview. Questions ranged from generic personality questions to some involving rating the appearance of 
girls.

As someone who has conducted many a interview in my short high school life, I've come to realise a few things which I think are
worth considering whenever conducting interviews.
